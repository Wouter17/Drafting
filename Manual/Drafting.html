On the Subject of Drafting

"You and me, we got this!"

This module follows the layout for the game League of Legends: a MOBA in which two teams of 5 battle against eachother.

League has 156 champions and you will need to pick which champions your team will play. Also known as drafting.

The column on the left represents the champions your team has picked.

The column on the right represents the champions the enemy team has picked.

The six buttons in the middle represent the champions you are allowed to choose from.

The draft happends in a set (phase) order:

  • 6 Bans
  • (Team) Left picks 1
  • (Team) Right picks 2
  • Left picks 2
  • Right picks 1
  • 4 Bans
  • Right picks 1
  • Left picks 2
  • Right picks 1

Determining the 6 Bans:

Take the alphabetical position of each of the champions on the buttons in reading order. Multiply them by their correspoding serial #, letters become their alphanumerical position with A=1.

Modulo each of the answers by 165 then add 1.

These are the indexes of the champions which have been banned and you may not pick these champions anymore.

You may now press any button to continue to the next phase.

Pick 2 & 3

Based on the role of the first champion picked, determine the roles of the second and third pick, using the following table:

First pick: Top Jungle Mid Bot Support
2nd pick Mid Bot Support Jungle Top
3th pick Jungle Mid Top Support Bot

Using the role determined from the table, your second pick will be the first champion with that role, with the same Class and Difficulty as the First pick.

Start searching downward in the Champions table (Appendix C) from the First pick until you find a champion to who this applies. If you reach the end of the table, wrap around to the beginning and continue. In all cases, only look at the first Role and Class mentioned.

If no champion matches these conditions, pick the first available champion in the determined role. Again, counting from the first pick.

Repeat this process for pick 3, but now use the 2nd row instead.


Determining the 4 Bans:

To determine the four bans, split the serial number up into three groups of two. Also create a group with all characters. Convert each of these groups from base 36 to base 10 (normal numbers).

For each of the answers, modulo it by the amount of champions and add 1.

Each of your answers represents the number of the champion that has been banned. If the champion is already picked or banned the next available champion in alphabetical order is banned instead.
